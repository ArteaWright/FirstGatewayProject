What it's all about

People often visit and immerse themselves into Charlotte to experience the city's new found complexion. Over the past decade Charlotte has grown over 58%. Becoming a melting pot of different people from different cities and countries and home to 8 Fortune 500 companies. The city's growth has brought great opportunities and space for innovation, but the root of the growth has come from an immense amount of incentive to develop and redevelop historic and significant places on the outskirts of uptown Charlotte.

The growth is largely accepted and new comers are always welcomed. However, with all of the new changes, it can be difficult to keep up with some of the best gems hidden in this growing city

To bring a solution this issue, a list of urban and beloved places and activities within Charlotte has been curated by those that live and grow around these establishments. Bringing together a great list of recommendation and suggestions from the most creditable sources...the community.
